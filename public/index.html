What is Willy-Nilly?

Willy-Nilly is a web app brought to life by the collaborative minds of Corey Richard, Wesley Hanson, Avram Adam and Brianna Herreras. It seeks to give you a more spontanous option to exploring a city.
Set your city and date you want to be spontanous and we will return random events happening in that time frame giving you the oppertunity to experience Willy-Nilly!
